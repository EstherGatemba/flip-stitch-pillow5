About us

Flip Pillow is a fortune500 company that was started in 2006 as a case project for enviroment protection. The project's goal was to bring pollution awareness into homes of New York State residents.

One of the most intimate and commonly used item in the home is the pillow. It offers comfort and is an item that can help display the style of its owner. The pillow can also hold sentimental value based on how it was acquired or by whom. This is why our pillows not only use sustainable fabrics and dyes our customers can customize the artwork.
